St. Anne de Beaupré School was founded in 1974 by Miss Aurora T. Apuada as St. Anne’s Special School to cater to the needs of children with special needs. Due to its efficient operations and constant improvement, the school gained recognition and was granted full recognition in 1980. In 1987, Ste. Anne de Beaupré School was established to mainstream special children, and it now offers recognized courses from preschool to high school. Its graduates have been admitted to prestigious universities and become professionals in various fields. The school is now on its 36th year and is known for its top-notch educational system and strict adherence to values formation.

Annean Vission and Mission

Ste. Anne de Beaupré School envisions a world of proactive Filipino leaders that make the world a better place both in the eyes of God and man.

Ste. Anne de Beaupré School offers a well-balanced educational program for the formation of individuals with moral virtues, high-level intelligence and functional skills.

Sainte Anne de Beaupre School is a private educational institution that caters to the needs of both regular and special children. Founded in 1974 by Miss Aurora T. Apuada, a Special Education (SPED) degree holder from the University of the Philippines, the school aims to provide a well-balanced educational program for the formation of individuals with moral virtues, high-level intelligence and functional skills.
